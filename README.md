# 📊 Profitability & Discount Impact Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes the Sample Superstore dataset to identify factors that influence company profitability, focusing on sales performance, discount impact, product categories, and loss-making products.

## Tools
- CSV
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Jupyter Notebook
- Power BI

## Key Insights
- Higher sales generally contribute to higher profit.
- High discounts tend to reduce profitability and may generate losses.
- Technology is the most profitable product category.
- Several products contribute significantly to overall losses.

## Recommendations
- Optimize discount strategies.
- Focus on high-profit categories.
- Review loss-making products.
- Balance sales growth with profitability goals.
